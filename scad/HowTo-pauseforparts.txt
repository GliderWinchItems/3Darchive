In Slic3r--

1.  Vizualization|Show Single Layer

Step to layer for insert and note layer number.

2.  Edit G-Code

3.  Search tab
    Enter: ;Layer change

4.  Search to step to insertion point

5.  Enter: M226 and M25, e.g.--

G1 X23.964 Y153.079 E36.73013
;Layer change
G1 E30.73013 F1800.00000
M226
M25
G92 E0

6.  When printing halts

  a.  Press Pause on panel
  b.  Insert parts
  c.  Press Continue on panel

  Don't insert parts without pressing Pause as
  it is easy to move the table and the x/y will
  be off for the remainder of the print.
